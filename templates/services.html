Super Crease (Permanent Creasing)

SuperCrease is a permanent creasing solution. Once we dry clean your garment and steam in the creases, we apply a specially formulated resin to creases from the inside of the trouser to make the crease permanent.

What this does for the garment is make the crease sharp, so when you wear it, it stands out confidently like its owner.

Guarantee: Permanent for life of garment | 50+ wash guarantee

Benefits of SuperCrease:

  • Increase your confidence level
  • Make better first impression
  • One time application
  • Safe with European certifications
  • Odorless & proven effective
